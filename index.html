Walrus Adventure

by Iulios Kouroupis

Somwehere in the Arctic lives a walrus who has no name.
He has no family, no friends, and most troubling of all, no place to call home.
As the polar caps rapidly melt, the walrus decides to withdraw all of the money from his bank account ($100) and spend it on a one way plane ticket.




When he arrives at the Arctic International Airport, the sign reads...
Paris - $140
Budapest - $120
New York City- $95
Santiago - $105
London - 200
St. Petersburg- $100
New Dehli - $230
